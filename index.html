Don't stop calling the FCC.

The FCC could save net neutrality, or destroy it; they're deciding right now. This page will connect you, at random, to the desk of an FCC employee (our goal is to blanket the agency with calls.) When they pick up, be polite and earnest–we want to persuade them–and say:

"I'm calling because I'm worried that Tom Wheeler will ignore our millions of comments for Title 2 because of his history as a cable company lobbyist. Please, do everything in your power to pass real net neutrality using Title 2.”

We'll connect you to a senior or mid-level employee at the FCC. If you like (and we recommend it!) you can choose to keep calling the FCC every day until they make their final decision. We won't use your phone number for anything else, we promise.

Here are some other fun things to ask the FCC:

  • Stop the Comcast / Time Warner Cable merger
  • Use Title II authority to reclassify the cable companies as utilities
  • Have a nice day!
